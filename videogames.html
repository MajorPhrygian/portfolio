Video Games

Video games are a fascinating collective creative experience. With hundreds of persons working on the same endeavor for years, they are nothing short of modern day cathedrals.
I am a project manager on Rainbow Six Siege, a first person shooter from Ubisoft that is now in its 8 year of live service. First person shooters are very competitive and sometimes, tempers flare up. Siege had gotten itself an undesirable reputation as having one of the most toxic community around. In the Player Protection cell, we are shifting this narrative by modyfing the game in ways that keep all the excitement, but makes the environment more positive. I'll showcase here some the work we have done.
